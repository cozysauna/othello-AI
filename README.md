# Othello AI

## How to Play
execute ```main.py```
```
$ python3 main.py
```
You can put a piece by inputting a number where a star exists (ex.32) 
``` 
   0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
00 . . . . . . ◯ ☆
10 . . . . ☆ . ◯ .
20 . . . ☆ ◯ ● ◯ ☆
30 . . ☆ ◯ ● . . .
40 . ☆ ◯ ● ◯ ☆ . .
50 . . ● . ☆ . . .
60 . . . . . . . .
70 . . . . . . . .
```

AI will put a piece about one second after you
You can finish the game if you input 'f'
